Community Guidelines for Pookie Babysitting App

At Pookie Babysitting App, we strive to create a safe and respectful online community for all our members. These guidelines are designed to ensure that our platform remains a positive and welcoming space for parents, caregivers, and childcare providers alike. By participating in our community, you agree to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Respect and Inclusion:

    • Treat others with kindness, empathy, and respect.
    • Avoid discriminatory or offensive language, including but not limited to race, ethnicity, gender, religion, or sexual orientation.
    • Embrace diversity and strive to create an inclusive environment for all community members.

  • Provide Accurate Information:

    • Ensure that information you share is truthful and accurate.
    • Do not mislead or provide false information to deceive others.

  • Parental Responsibility:

    • Parents and guardians are responsible for their children's online activities within our community.
    • Ensure that any content shared, or interactions conducted by children are appropriate and align with these guidelines.

  • Use Constructive Communication:

    • Engage in open and constructive discussions.
    • Be mindful of your language and tone to foster positive interactions.
    • Refrain from personal attacks, harassment, or bullying of any kind

  • No Promotional or Spam Content:

    • Do not use the platform for self-promotion, advertising, or spamming.
    • Refrain from posting or sharing irrelevant or repetitive content.

  • Protect Privacy and Confidentiality:

    • Respect the privacy and confidentiality of others.
    • Do not share personal information, including contact details, addresses, or financial information, without consent.

  • Report Suspicious or Inappropriate Content:

    • If you come across any content that violates these guidelines or appears suspicious, report it to our moderation team.
    • Make use of the reporting features on our platform to flag any issues.

  • Compliance with Legal and Safety Standards:

    • Abide by all applicable laws and regulations when using our platform.
    • Do not participate in any activities that pose threats to the safety, security, or well-being of others.

1What is the babysitting mobile application all about?

The babysitting mobile application is an innovative platform that connects qualified, trustworthy babysitters and tutors with parents looking for reliable childcare services.

2 How does the application work?

The application works by allowing parents to search for available babysitters in their area, view their profiles, and book them directly through the app. Babysitters can also create profiles, set their rates, availability, and accept or decline bookings.

3Am I able to view the profiles of the babysitters before booking them?

Yes, you can view the babysitter's profile, send a message or arrange a call via the app to get to know the person before booking them. It is important for the parents to ask questions and get to know the babysitters as much as possible until they are satisfied.

5 How do you ensure the safety of my child?

The safety of your child is our top priority. Before going live, all sitters are required to send in their Passport/ID documents which are reviewed by the Pookie Team. Additional Garda vetting is carried out as per the sitters wishes. This is highly recommended by pookie to all childcare professionals on the app. We also allow parents to view ratings and reviews from other users to ensure a positive experience.

6 How much does it cost to use the babysitting app?

Pookie has a Freemium and Premium rate for both Parents, babysitters and other childcare professionals. For Parents, Pookie Freemium is free, we only charge a €4.99 booking fee when you book a sitter via the App. Pookie Premium is a €9.99 recurring monthly subscription fee. For Childcare professionals including babysitter, Pookie Freemium is free, we only charge a €1.99 platform fee, and Pookie Premium is a €4.99 recurring monthly subscription fee. Check out our price plans for more information and services included in the plans.

7 Do you provide emergency babysitting services?

Yes, we offer emergency babysitting services for those last-minute situations when you need a babysitter urgently. We aim to provide a babysitter within a few hours if possible. This is not via the App and involves signing up for emergency baby-sitting plan.

8 Can I request a specific babysitter?

Yes, you can request a specific babysitter if they are available. You can view their profile and ratings before booking them. You can add a babysitter to your favourites to keep booking them whenever you need.

9 What are the rates for babysitting services?

The rates for babysitting services vary depending on the babysitter, number of children, and hours required. Pookie does not set the rate/hr it is up to the babysitter to do so.

10 What payment options do you offer?

We offer multiple payment options, including credit card, debit card, and PayPal. Payment is processed securely through the app, and you will receive a receipt for each transaction. Pookie does not accept cash payments for any transaction.

11 if my babysitter is running late, what do I do?

Use the messaging tool in the app to contact them and find out their location. Hopefully, they will reach out to you first! Please keep in mind that you will only pay the sitter for the actual time they spend sitting. Therefore, if they arrive late, you have the flexibility to adjust the start time when making the payment in the end. If you urgently need to find another person, quickly post a job to find available replacements within minutes.

12 What happens if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel a booking up to 24 hours in advance without penalty. If you cancel within 24 hours of the booking, you will be charged a cancellation fee of 20% of the total booking. This money goes to the sitter as it is only fair on them.

13 What happens if a babysitter cancels at the last minute?

If a babysitter cancels last minute, quickly post a job to see if there is other available sitters nearby. We advise sitters not to cancel last minute as they can possibly get blacklisted. But there are unavoidable situations like Sickness, or bad weather that can make things inevitable.

14 What if I have a problem with a babysitter?

If you have a problem with a babysitter, please contact us immediately.at support@pookieapp.ie or send us a chat on our website and we will respond as quickly as we can. We will investigate the issue and take appropriate action. We strive to provide excellent customer service and ensure that every experience is a positive one.

15 What services does Pookie babysitting application offer?

Pookie offers a range of services including occasional babysitting, regular scheduled babysitting, and emergency babysitting. We also offer tutors for that extra help with your child’s education

16 Are the babysitters trained and experienced?

Yes, all our babysitters have training or/and experienced in childcare. Their identity is verified upon sign up and further garda vetting is done if they do not have one as per our recommendation. However, it is important to know that not all babysitters have a Garda vetting, and it is up to the parent's discretion if they want to book them. All Garda Vetted sitters will have a Vetting icon on displayed on their profile.

17 What age group of children do you cater to?

We cater to a wide range of age groups, from infants to toddlers, preschoolers to early teens.

18 Are there babysitters that speak other languages?

A babysitter will specify in their profile if they speak any other languages asides from English. A lot of babysitters are bilingual, this can benefit a child if you desire them to be bi-lingual. Currently, we prioritise the following languages; French, Italian, Spanish, German, Polish, Arabic, Chinese, Greek, Russian, Portuguese, Swahili.

19 How do I book a babysitter?

You can book a babysitter via the app. Download the app, create your profile, search for a babysitter, use our in-app messaging to start a conversation, and when you are satisfied go ahead and book the sitter via the app

20What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellation. If cancellation is made within 24 hours, there would be a 20% cancellation fee. This goes to the sitter as it is unfair for them losing potential earnings.

Here are some tips for parents before a babysitting job starts:

  • Communicate with the babysitter beforehand: Before the babysitting job begins, have a conversation with the babysitter to discuss expectations, routines, and any specific instructions or requirements for your child. This will help ensure that the babysitter is well-prepared and can provide appropriate care for your child.

  • Share important information: Provide the babysitter with important information such as emergency contact numbers, your address, any allergies or medical conditions your child has, and any other relevant details. It's important for the babysitter to have easy access to this information in case of an emergency.

  • Leave emergency numbers: Provide the babysitter with a list of emergency numbers, including your contact information, a trusted next of kin’s or Adult’s contact number and address. Make sure the babysitter knows how to call for help in case of an emergency.

  • Show trust and respect: It's important to show trust and respect to the babysitter. Make them feel comfortable and confident in their role. Let them know that you appreciate their help and that you have confidence in their ability to take care of your child.

  • Leave a detailed schedule: Provide the babysitter with a detailed schedule for the time they'll be caring for your child. Include information about feeding times, nap times, medication schedules (if applicable), and any other important routines or activities.

  • Show the babysitter around: Take some time to show the babysitter around your home. Point out the location of important items such as first aid kits, fire extinguishers, and emergency exits. Familiarize the babysitter with your child's bedroom, play areas, and any other relevant areas in your home.

  • Make sure the home is safe: Before leaving, ensure that your home is safe and childproofed. Remove any hazards or potentially dangerous items from your child's reach. Take precautions such as locking cabinets with cleaning supplies or medications.

  • Provide entertainment options: Leave age-appropriate toys, games, and books for the babysitter to engage your child in activities. This ensures that there are safe and enjoyable options for them to play with.

  • Confirm transportation arrangements: If the babysitter will be taking your child anywhere during the babysitting job, confirm transportation arrangements. Make sure the babysitter has a car seat or any other necessary equipment for safe transportation.

Remember, always trust your gut instincts when choosing a caregiver for your child. If something doesn't feel right, explore other options on the app or look for alternatives. Safety and your child's well-being should be the utmost priority.
