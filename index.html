Whether it's work, business or a short trip, you deserve the assurance that your kid is in the right hands. Providing a trusted and affordable service that connect parents with local babysitter and other childcare needs.

How Pookie works?

Registration and Profile Creation

Users create an account and provide essential details to build their profile with Pookie.

Search and Matchmaking

Pookie helps parents find and connect with suitable babysitters based on their preferences and requirements.

Booking and Payment

In the booking and payment process, parents request babysitting services through the app, and payment is facilitated either through integrated payment gateways or in-person cash transactions.
